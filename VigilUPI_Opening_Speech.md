# VigilUPI — Opening Speech
### Steve Jobs Style · ~3 Minutes

---

*[Walk to center. Pause. Look at the audience. Don't rush.]*

---

Every morning, 500 million Indians wake up and send money on UPI.

To their landlord. To their mother. To the chai wala down the street.

It's seamless. It's instant. It's beautiful.

*[Pause.]*

And last year — ₹11,333 crore of it was stolen.

---

Now, the banks will tell you they have security.

OTPs. PINs. Device binding.

And they're right. They do.

*[Beat.]*

But here's the thing nobody talks about —

By the time the OTP arrives... the attacker already has your phone.

By the time the PIN is entered... it's their finger doing it.

The system did exactly what it was designed to do.

It just couldn't tell the difference between *you* — and the person pretending to be you.

*[Pause. Let it land.]*

---

So we asked a different question.

Not *"is this the right device?"*

Not *"is this the right PIN?"*

We asked —

**"Is this the right *person*?"**

---

Turns out, every person holds their phone differently.

You have a rhythm when you type. A pressure when you tap. A steadiness — or a nervousness — in how you grip the phone.

These patterns are invisible to you.

But they are *yours.*

As unique as your fingerprint. As personal as your voice.

*[Pause.]*

We call it your **Soulprint™.**

---

VigilUPI watches for it. Silently. Continuously. On your device — never in the cloud.

And the moment something feels wrong —

the moment the rhythm breaks, the grip changes, the context shifts —

it stops the transaction.

Not after. Not with a sorry email three days later.

*Right then.*

---

We built this in 48 hours.

Not because it was easy.

Because it was necessary.

*[Pause. Smile.]*

Let us show you what we built.

---

*[Turn to screen.]*

---

*— VigilUPI · CTU Hackathon*
